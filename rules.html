Rules & Regulations

1. General Court Rules

  • Only registered users are allowed to book a court.
  • Players must wear proper sports attire and enter the court bare foot.
  • Food and drinks (except water) are not allowed inside the court.
  • Respect other players and avoid unnecessary disruptions.

2. Booking & Cancellation

  • Each player can book a maximum of one slot per day.
  • Bookings must be made at least 10 minutes before the slot starts.
  • If you cannot attend, cancel your booking at least 30 minutes before.
  • Repeated no-shows may lead to temporary suspension.

3. Gameplay Etiquette

  • Each match is limited to 60 minutes.
  • Follow fair play rules and resolve disputes amicably.
  • Bring your personal shuttles; it won't be provided.

4. Code of Conduct

  • Any kind of misconduct, violence, or disrespect will result in an immediate ban.
  • Maintain a friendly sportsmanship spirit.
  • Follow the guidance of the court staff at all times.
  • DO not use Cell phone
  • You are under CC TV surveillance
  • No shouting
  • No Group sitting and disturbing
  • No Bettings
